About the Disaster

In recent weeks, unprecedented monsoon rains have caused severe flooding across multiple districts in Punjab, Pakistan. The flooding has resulted in significant damage to infrastructure, homes, and agricultural land.

Over 2 million people have been affected, with many losing their homes, livelihoods, and access to basic necessities. The situation continues to be critical as water levels remain high and more rain is forecasted.

Our mission is to provide immediate relief to those affected by distributing food, clean water, medical supplies, and temporary shelter. With your support, we can help the people of Punjab recover from this devastating natural disaster.

Frequently Asked Questions

How will my donation be used?

Your donation will be used to provide immediate relief to flood victims, including food, clean water, medical supplies, and temporary shelter. We ensure that over 90% of all donations go directly to relief efforts.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, Punjab Relief is a registered nonprofit organization, and all donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a receipt for your records.

How can I volunteer to help?

We welcome volunteers! Please click on the "Volunteer Now" button above and fill out the application form. We'll contact you with available opportunities based on your skills and the current needs.

How can I get updates on your relief efforts?

We regularly update our website and social media channels with information about our relief efforts. You can also sign up for our newsletter to receive updates directly to your inbox.
